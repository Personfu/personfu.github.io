Global Threat Landscape (2026)

The Spider Faction: Global eCrime Syndicates

The Spider faction represents the decentralized, profit-driven underbelly of global cybercrime, operating primarily as Ransomware-as-a-Service (RaaS) affiliates, Initial Access Brokers (IABs), and data extortionists. They employ robust quadruple extortion models and aggressive DDoS tactics.

High-Tier Spiders

BITWISE SPIDER: (Ext) The apex predator of RaaS, targeting 44 industries. [LockBitSupp, Fox William Mulder]
REVENANT SPIDER: (Ext) Highly sophisticated rust-based payloads. [Stinkbug, Qilin]
GRACEFUL SPIDER: (High) Masters of zero-day mass exploitation. [Clop, FIN11]
BRAIN SPIDER: (High) Relentless and noisy data leaks. [8BASE, Phobos]
TRAVELING SPIDER: (High) Broad global reach, aggressive negotiations. [Nokoyawa]

The Bear Vanguard: Russian State-Sponsored Operations

The Bear faction represents the absolute pinnacle of state-sponsored cyber warfare, emulating the deeply resourced intelligence services of the Russian Federation. They excel in PsyOps, critical infrastructure sabotage, and ultimate stealth.

High-Tier Bears

COZY BEAR: (Ext) Ultimate stealth operators. Masters of supply chain compromises. [APT29, NOBELIUM]
FANCY BEAR: (Ext) Highly aggressive intelligence gatherers. [APT28, Sofacy]
VENOMOUS BEAR: (Ext) Multi-stage malware & satellite IP hijacks. [Turla]
VOODOO BEAR: (Ext) Premier sabotage unit, destructive wipers. [Sandworm]

The Chollima Bureau: North Korean Hybrid Operations

Unlike other state-sponsored groups, Chollima has a dual mandate: steal classified intelligence AND generate massive revenue to bypass global sanctions. You face nation-state zero-days mixed with the aggressive financial motivations of eCrime.

High-Tier Chollimas

LABYRINTH CHOLLIMA: (Ext) Billion-dollar financial heists, complex supply chain compromises. [Lazarus Group]
STARDUST CHOLLIMA: (Ext) Exclusively focused on targeting global financial institutions & SWIFT. [APT38]

The Panda Nexus: Chinese Cyber Espionage

The ultimate vacuum cleaners of digital data, focused entirely on large-scale intellectual property theft, deep telecommunications surveillance, and securing strategic economic advantages.

High-Tier Pandas

MUSTANG PANDA: (Ext) Lightning-fast exploitation of public vulnerabilities. [BRONZE PRESIDENT]
HOLLOW PANDA: (Ext) Long-term, deep-cover intelligence gathering.

The Kitten Vanguard: Iranian State-Aligned Operations

Highly aggressive, ideologically driven, and deeply disruptive. They frequently prioritize speed, psychological impact, and visible disruption over absolute stealth, often deploying devastating wiper malware.

High-Tier Kittens

BANISHED KITTEN: (Ext) Devastating wiper attacks and psychological operations. [Homeland Justice]
IMPERIAL KITTEN: (High) Heavily focused on defense industrial base and IT logistics. [APT35, Tortoiseshell]

The Jackals & Emerging State Threats

Ideologically driven hacktivist collectives (Jackals) providing pure chaos via DDoS and hack-and-leaks. Emerging APTS from developing nations (India, Pakistan, Belarus, Egypt) bring customized remote access trojans focused on regional targets.
PARTISAN JACKAL (Belarus): (Destructive) Hacktivist group conducting highly impactful destructive operations. [Cyber Partisans]
BOUNTY JACKAL (Russia): (Heavy DDoS) Pro-state hacktivist group. [NoName057(16)]
HAZY TIGER (India): (Mid) Aggressive regional espionage. [Bitter APT]
MYTHIC LEOPARD (Pakistan): (Mid) Heavy focus on military espionage. [Transparent Tribe]
