// OPERATIVE BRIEFING

Welcome to CYBERWORLD — the unified FLLC operative platform. This is where the Oregon Trail meets cybersecurity: you traverse a hostile network, node by node, solving real CTF and Hackathon challenges to earn XP and survive to the final exfil point.

The platform combines the CTF Trail gauntlet (10 CTF + 10 Hackathon challenges), the War Games training terminal, the Cyber Arcade mini-games, and the full RPG World MMORPG — all under one roof. Every challenge has hints, walkthroughs, and real-world cybersecurity education built in.

Choose your mode. Stay frosty. FLLC operatives never leave a flag behind.
